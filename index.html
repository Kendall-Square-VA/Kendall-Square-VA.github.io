Luxury Living in
Fairfax, VA

Located near Fairfax Circle, the Kendall Square community is a group of 4-story Luxury Townhomes that were created in 2008 by Stanley Martin. Convenient to the Metro, these homes put you steps away from some of the most popular places in the region.

Not Just a House

It's a home. A community. The people of Kendall Square strive to make living together a neighborhood that everyone can be proud of.

Perfect Balance

Kendall square offers the perfect balance between city excitement and suburban life by placing you within walking distance to the Metro and a short drive to several major highways.
