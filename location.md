## Close and Convenient

Kendall Square is a luxury townhome community located near **Fairfax Circle** — the intersection of
Lee Highway and Fairfax Boulevard in Fairfax, Virginia. Vienna Metro Station (Orange/Silver Line)
is just **1.2 miles away**, making a trip downtown as easy as hopping on the train.

- Less than **2 miles** from I-66
- Less than **4 miles** from I-495 / Capital Beltway
- Quick access to Tysons Corner, Reston, and Washington D.C.
- Minutes from Mosaic District dining and entertainment

## Driving Directions

From **I-495**, take I-66 West to **VA-243 / Nutley Street (Exit 62)**. Turn right onto
**Lee Highway / Route 29 South**. Turn right onto **Blake Lane**, then left onto
**Kingsbridge Drive**. The community entrance is immediately to the left on **Beech Grove Drive**.
