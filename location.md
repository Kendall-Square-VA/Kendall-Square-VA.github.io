## Close and Convenient
Kendall Square is a luxury town home community located near Fairfax Circle (the intersection of Lee Highway and 
Fairfax Boulevard). Vienna Metro Station is just 1.2 miles away, making a trip downtown as easy as hopping on the 
train. It's less than two miles from I-66 and less than 4 miles from I-495, making it convenient to get to from 
anywhere in the Metro-DC area.

## Directions
From I-495 take I-66 West to VA243 (exit 62) onto Nutley Street. Turn right onto Lee Highway/Route-29 South. Next, 
turn right onto Blake Lane, followed by a left onto Kingsbridge Drive. The entrance to the community is immediately 
to the left onto Beech Grove Drive.
